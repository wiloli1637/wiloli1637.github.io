Ballet is the base of all dance, it is the form and elegance that is set to make all dances look clean and beautiful. Ballet has its own vocabulary and has a wide range of moves and ways the moves are carried out. The level of ballet you take depds on your flexibility and mental strength. Ballet requires focus to ensure that no harm is caused to the body when executing the moves. Dancers usually wear tights and leotards to class. Teachers are very strict on uniform and making sure the hair is tied in a tight bun on the crown of the head.
